HOW ELECTIONS WORK Any citizen of Queensland is able to and holds power to participate in voting, either by running themselves, or being a voter. Runners have the option of making a case as to why they should be the role their running for. If they do, it must be no more and no less than One Paragraph. DAY 1 Campaigns open for 24 hours DAY 2 There will be a large message with the candidates and their cases. On the second day, an interview may take place if the runners decide to do so DAY 3 On the 3rd day people can vote with either 1️⃣, or 2️⃣, any person running cannot vote for themselves or anyone else, making it completely citizen based. Ties will result in an extension of one day, and as a final tiebreaker, I will make the decision if necessary, based off the fluency, capabilities, and promises stated in their cases. Results After the election is finished, the results will be removed from this channel, and either archived, or deleted. The winner will then receive the role they ran for. REMEMBER, no one has the right to influence your vote other than the candidates and their cases. (edited)
